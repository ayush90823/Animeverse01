Season 1

This season introduces us to Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the Hokage.

Season 2

Season 2 continues Naruto's journey and introduces more characters like Gaara and the Sand Ninja.

Season 3

In Season 3, Naruto and his team take on new missions, including facing the dangerous Sound Ninja.

Season 4

Season 4 focuses on the Chūnin Exams and the start of the invasion of Konoha.

Season 5

Season 5 introduces more intense battles, including Naruto's conflict with the powerful Orochimaru.

Season 6

Season 6 features the ongoing battle against Orochimaru and his attempts to conquer Konoha.

Season 7

Season 7 includes the continuation of the search for Sasuke and Naruto’s struggle with his feelings of abandonment.

Season 8

Season 8 begins to explore the story behind the Akatsuki organization and Naruto's journey to find Sasuke.

Season 9

Season 9 concludes Naruto's journey with intense battles and the ultimate showdown with his greatest enemies.
